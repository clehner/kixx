Welcome to the first pre-release of Kixx Bootstrapper version 0.1!

Warning: Pre-Release software ahead:

Brought to you by The Fireworks Project

The Kixx Bootstrapper is an extension designed for the Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome web browsers that provides a plugin platform for a modular system of applications called "toolpacks".

The main goal of Kixx is to provide a simple means of installing and importing internal code libraries and user facing toolpack applications. The philosophy behind Kixx is to keep everything modular, facilitating faster and more innovative development of more complex internet enabled software systems than what the host browsers provide on their own.

It is important to note that Kixx is in the very early stages of development, and what you are looking at here is the 0.1 pre-release alpha version. To date there is no way to install or use Kixx on the Google Chrome browser, but plans are underway to add that capability. Kixx is an open source software project, and is inviting users and developers to get involved in the early stages of the project. Kixx has the potential to shape the way we use the internet in the future, but without your input, it will remain a small fringe project. Kixx needs comments, concerns, and requests from users, as well as contributions from developers and designers.
